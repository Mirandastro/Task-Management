Introduction

Welcome to GTLM (Group Task List Management). We are committed to respecting your privacy and want to make sure that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. We collect the following types of information:

1. Personal Information: This includes your email address and password used during the sign-up process.

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third-party services for their marketing purposes.

We implement strong security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We use cookies to store session information to facilitate remembering your login information, and to remember your settings.

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. You can do this by logging into your account settings. If you have any issues or questions, please contact us.
